# AI Research Prompt Framework

## 🎯 Objective
Perform structured, deep research using AI tools.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Template

Act as a research analyst.

Break down the topic: "{TOPIC}"

Steps:
1. Define key subtopics
2. Identify trends and insights
3. Extract important data points
4. Summarize findings

Requirements:
- Be structured and clear
- Avoid generic statements
- Focus on insights

---

## 📊 Output
- Research summary
- Key insights
- Actionable conclusions
