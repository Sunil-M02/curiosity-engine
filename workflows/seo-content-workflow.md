# SEO Content Workflow (AI-Powered)

## 🎯 Objective
Create high-quality, EEAT-compliant, human-like SEO content using AI tools.

---

## 🔍 Step 1: Keyword Research
- Identify primary keyword (high intent)
- Extract secondary + long-tail keywords
- Analyze SERP competitors

---

## 🧠 Step 2: Search Intent Mapping
- Informational / Transactional / Commercial
- Identify what user actually wants
- Map content angle accordingly

---

## 🧩 Step 3: Topic Clustering
- Group keywords into clusters
- Build topical authority
- Avoid keyword cannibalization

---

## ✍️ Step 4: AI Content Generation
Tools:
- Claude → reasoning + structure
- GPT → expansion + variations

Process:
- Generate outline first
- Expand section by section
- Maintain natural tone

---

## 🧑‍💻 Step 5: Humanization Layer
- Remove AI patterns
- Add real-world examples
- Improve readability

---

## ⚙️ Step 6: SEO Optimization
- Add meta title + description
- Optimize headings (H1–H3)
- Internal linking

---

## 📊 Output
- SEO blog article
- Optimized metadata
- Structured content ready to publish


## 🔧 Improvement Notes
- Optimized for EEAT and AEO guidelines
- Focus on human-like output
