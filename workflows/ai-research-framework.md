# AI Research Framework

## 🎯 Objective
Use AI tools to perform structured, high-quality research efficiently.

---

## 🔍 Step 1: Define Research Goal
- What problem are you solving?
- What output is required?

---

## 🧠 Step 2: Query Structuring
- Break topic into sub-questions
- Use step-by-step prompts

---

## ⚙️ Step 3: AI Execution
Tools:
- Claude → deep reasoning
- GPT → data expansion

---

## 📊 Step 4: Data Structuring
- Summarize findings
- Extract key insights
- Remove redundancy

---

## 🧑‍💻 Step 5: Validation
- Cross-check facts
- Verify important claims

---

## 📈 Output
- Structured research document
- Key insights
- Actionable conclusions
