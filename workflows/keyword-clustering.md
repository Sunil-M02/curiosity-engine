# Keyword Clustering Framework

## 🎯 Objective
Group keywords into meaningful clusters to build topical authority and improve SEO rankings.

---

## 🔍 Step 1: Collect Keywords
Sources:
- Google Search Console
- Keyword tools
- Competitor analysis

---

## 🧠 Step 2: Identify Intent
Group keywords by:
- Informational
- Commercial
- Transactional

---

## 🧩 Step 3: Cluster Formation
- Group similar keywords
- Map to single page or topic
- Avoid duplication

---

## ⚙️ Step 4: Content Mapping
- Assign each cluster to:
  - Blog post
  - Landing page
  - Supporting article

---

## 📊 Output
- Keyword clusters
- Content plan
- SEO structure
